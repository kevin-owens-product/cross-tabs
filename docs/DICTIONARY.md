## Insight Gurus

### Who are they?

- Skilled experts working in data, research and insights roles
- Obsessed with data accuracy and data value
- They are skilled in digging in the data and finding trends
- Highly number literate; a table of data speaks to them

### What do they do?

- Leverage data to support better business decision-making
- Currently act as gatekeepers, supplying the rest of their organization with insights, answers and solutions from data partners like GWI
- Make sure colleagues can make evidence-based decisions

TL;DR **Market Analysts** from companies.
